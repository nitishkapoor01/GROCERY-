Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I place an order?

You can browse our menu, select your favorite food items, and proceed to checkout.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and cash on delivery (COD).

3. How can I track my order?

Go to "My Orders" in your account to track the real-time status of your order.

4. What if I receive the wrong order?

If you receive an incorrect order, contact our support team within 30 minutes for a resolution.
